Comparing image synthesis methods

In this study, we are comparing different methods for images synthesis. First, you are presented a real image from a dataset, followed by two generated images created by 2 different methods.
Please click on the one that you find more visually similar to the real image. If you cannot make up your mind, pick the last answer.
A new group of evaluation is shown after each click, as an endless stream. You can stop at any time.
